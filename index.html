Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about FitZone membership

What are your operating hours?
We're open 24/7, 365 days a year! Our staffed hours are Monday-Friday 5am-11pm, Saturday-Sunday 6am-10pm. Outside these hours, members have 24/7 access with their membership card.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! All membership plans include a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You can access all facilities and attend group classes during your trial period.
Can I freeze my membership?
Absolutely! You can freeze your membership for up to 3 months per year. There's a small administrative fee of $10/month during the freeze period. Just give us 7 days notice.
What should i bring to my first visit?
Bring comfortable workout clothes, athletic shoes, a water bottle, and a towel. We provide lockers (bring your own lock), and Elite members receive complimentary towel service.
